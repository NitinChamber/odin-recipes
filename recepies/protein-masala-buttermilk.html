Refreshing Masala Buttermilk - A Spiced Indian Summer Cooler with 15g Protein

Fuel your day with this deliciously refreshing and protein-rich Masala Buttermilk! This twist on the traditional Indian chaas blends the cooling goodness of buttermilk with the power of roasted chickpeas, delivering both flavor and nutrition in every sip. Lightly spiced with classic masala ingredients like cumin, ginger, and coriander, this drink is not only soothing to the stomach but also surprisingly fillingâ€”making it a perfect mid-morning snack or post-workout refreshment.

With 15 grams of protein per serving, this upgraded chaas supports muscle recovery and keeps hunger at bay. The roasted chickpeas add a nutty depth and a gentle crunch when blended, giving the drink a creamy yet hearty texture. Whether you're looking for a gut-friendly beverage or a unique way to boost your protein intake naturally, this Masala Buttermilk is a must-try!

Ingredients

  • Roasted chickpeas: 50g
  • Curd: 1 cup
  • Water: 1.5 cup
  • Green Chilli: 1 piece
  • Mint leaves: 8-10 pieces
  • Coriander: 1/4 cup
  • Asafoetida: 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin pownder: 1 tsp
  • Salt: As per taste
  • Ginger: As per taste
  • Ice Cubes: 5-8 cubes

Steps

  1. Add roasted chickpeas, green chilli, mint leaves, coriander, asafoetida, cumin powder, salt & ginger in a mixer grinder jar and make a fine power out of it.

  2. Once the mixture is grinded into fine powder, add curd, water & ice cubes into the grinder that contains the powder and mix all the ingredients.

Voila!! Your delicious protein packed masala buttermilk is ready, serve it chilled.
