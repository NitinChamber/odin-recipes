Multivitamin Smoothie

Picture credit: www.google.com

Packed with Daily Goodness - Your Multivitamin Boost in a Glass!

This multivitamin smoothie is a rich, creamy blend of nutrient-dense ingredients designed to nourish your body and energize your day. Cashews and almonds form a silky base, offering healthy fats, protein, and essential minerals. Figs and dates add natural sweetness along with fiber, iron, and antioxidants, while pumpkin seeds bring in a boost of magnesium and zinc for immune and metabolic support. Banana enhances the texture and adds potassium for heart and muscle health, and a touch of jaggery lends a warm, unrefined sweetness that ties it all together. Blended with milk or water to suit your taste, this smoothie is a wholesome, satisfying way to get your daily dose of vitamins and minerals—straight from real, whole foods.

Ingredients

  • Cashew: 5-6 pieces
  • Almonds: 5-6 pieces
  • Pumpkin seeds: 1 tsp
  • Dates: 2 pieces
  • Figs: 2 pieces
  • Banana: 1
  • Jaggery powder: As required
  • Cumin pownder: 1 tsp
  • Milk / Water: As per taste & requirenment

Steps

  1. Soak the cashews, almonds, and pumpkin seeds in water overnight in one bowl, and in a separate bowl, soak the dates and figs.

  2. Once soaked, add the cashews, almonds, pumpkin seeds, figs, and dates to a blender along with a ripe banana, a small piece of jaggery, and either milk or water, depending on your taste preference. Blend everything together until smooth and creamy.

Voila! A glass full of natural energy, rich flavor, and pure nourishment—ready to be enjoyed fresh.
